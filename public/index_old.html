Welcome to Finland

Congratulations for having been accepted to study in Finland! From our own experience we know that you're super excited to jump on that plane and fly to Helsinki, but there are a few things you should know. Due to Finnish systems and regulations, you have a high chance of running into issues with your current bank. Below you see an overview of these.

I have a European Euro account.

In most cases, you will have little problems, however, when you want to do more than pay for your beers, you might run into problems.

  • Some bank cards don't work
  • It might be difficult to receive salary
  • And more...

I have an other account.

Using your current bank account in Finland will most likely cost you a lot of money make you feel miserable. You can expect problems like these:

  • You will have transaction fees and limits
  • Many bank cards don't work
  • Receiving salary is not possible
  • And more...

In many cases it's a good idea to open a Finnish bank account. We help you to do that.

How Alice works

  1. We give you an overview of the different offers from Finnish banks.
    Everything in simple, understandable English.
  2. You can tell us what's important to you and we give you a good recommendation.
    No secret deals with banks. You can click the link and verify the specifications yourself.
  3. You fill in your personal details and upload some required documents.
    We use those only for opening your account. Everything is encrypted, no hacks, no NSA stuff.
  4. We take care of your application for a bank account.
    Paperwork? Proving your student status? Phone calls with the bank? No, you don't have to do a thing.
  5. You choose where you want to pick up your account package.
    We make sure everything is there before your arrival. You just have to show your passport and you're done!
  6. That's it!
    Because you've arranged all this a few months before your arrival, the banks have had enough time to verify that you're not a criminal, so no need to wait for netbanking or other services.
